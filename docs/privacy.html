Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Overview

Durak ("the App") is a card game developed by Yaroslav Teper. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage data or analytics are gathered
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used
  • No user accounts are required
  • No data is sent over the internet

Internet Connection

The App functions entirely offline and does not require or use an internet connection.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any user, including children. It is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
