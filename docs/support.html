Durak - Support

About the App

Durak is a classic Russian card game where you play against an AI opponent. The game supports both English and Russian languages and features smart AI that adapts its strategy throughout the game.

How to Play

  • The game uses a 36-card deck (6 through Ace)
  • The bottom card of the deck determines the trump suit (marked with a red border)
  • The player with the lowest trump card attacks first
  • Attackers play cards; defenders must beat them with higher cards of the same suit or any trump card
  • If you can't defend, press "Take" to pick up all cards
  • Press "Done" when you're finished attacking
  • The first player to empty their hand after the deck runs out wins

Features

  • Play in English or Russian
  • Drag or tap mode for playing cards
  • Smart AI opponent with card counting
  • Sound effects with mute option
  • Works on iPhone and iPad
  • Fully offline - no internet required

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I switch between drag and tap mode?
A: Open the Menu (top left corner) and choose Drag or Tap under Mode.

Q: How do I mute the sound?
A: Open the Menu and tap "Sound: On" to toggle it off.

Q: Can I change the language?
A: Open the Menu, select "Main Menu", and choose your language on the intro screen.
